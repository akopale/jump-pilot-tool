The JUMP Framework

Judgment, Understanding, Mentorship, and Professional Growth — a coaching-based approach that combines AI-enabled delivery, structured reflection, and mentor feedback to accelerate the development of professional judgment and core technical skills.

Task Roadmap Tool

This tool supports the JUMP pilot by guiding doer through a structured planning process before starting a task, and giving reviewers a consistent framework for coaching conversations.

How the pilot works
1
Initial task assignment & check-in
Doer and reviewer align on the task, available tools, and objectives before work begins — the same way they typically would for any task. This step happens outside of this tool — no action required here.
Reviewer + doer
2
Complete task roadmap · 10–15 min
Before starting work, the doer fills out a structured plan covering the task, data sources, AI tools, expected outcome, and any questions or concerns.
Doer
3
Review roadmap · ~10 min
Reviewer looks over the doer's plan and adds comments on each section to prepare for the coaching conversation.
Reviewer
4
Discussion · 15–30 min
Doer and reviewer meet to discuss the plan side by side. Focus on alignment, open questions, coaching, and how the doer plans to use AI tools.
Reviewer + doer
5
Review deliverable & discuss lessons learned
With alignment established, the doer executes the task using AI tools as planned. Sources and assumptions are documented along the way.
Reviewer + doer
